ABOUT US

In 2012, Wishberry premiered the concept of crowdfunding in India for Independent filmmakers. Over 400 media projects have raised over 2 million dollars using the platform's rewards-based crowdfunding model from over 15,000 people across the world.

Leveraging on its experience in supporting indie cinema, Wishberry is now extending its digital platform to allow High Net Worth Individuals (HNIs) to discover and invest in low budget cinema in exchange for a share of the film’s profits.
